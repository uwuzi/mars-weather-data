Daily Mars Weather Data

GitHub page

Data is obtained from NASA's Insight Mission API. Measurements are taken once daily, but the data available is limited to the past week (a Martian "Sol" = an Earth day).

I want to document the history of the weather data, so I will be storing it and plotting it here. I hope to keep adding as long as they continue to make it available. The data starts with December 9th, 2019 (Sol 368).

You can view the raw JSON data from the navbar button above or at data.uwuzi.com.

Or run: $curl -L data.uwuzi.com
